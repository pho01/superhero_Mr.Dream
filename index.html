The event first happened in late 1996, a woman’s sweet dream was interrupted while she was sleeping at the midnight, then, she saw her sweet dream was eaten by a human-like something. After that, more and more similar situations were reported to the police successively. Therefore, National Security Agency assigned special detective PeiJung to deal with this freak condition. 21 years have passed away though, Mr.Dream has not even caught once. However, detective PeiJung gets the profound understanding toward Mr.Dream based on the 21 years research.
STORY
Mr.Dream was a normal person like all of us a century ago. At the year he was 19, he did not sleep for three days in order to get through the final in his freshman year. When he finally got a chance to rest, he fell into the a deep deep sleep. Then, he lost in his own dream. He was looking for the way out for seven years. One day, he hear from an old priest that the only link between real world and the dream is the Moon. He spent another two years camping onto the highest mountain and reached where the Moon is. Afterward, he signed a contract with the Moon for going back to the reality. The cost is that he has to work for Moon rest of his life. Therefore, he became a postman who is responsible for delivering the dreams to thousands of millions of human being every night. While Mr.Dream is on duty, he wears a human-skin suit which enables him implement his job. After 50 years, Mr.Dream started to be tired of such an immutable life. He broke the rule which in the contract with Moon, abandoning the postman job and living depend on the energy from human’s dreams.
CITY OF RESIDENCE
Mr.Dream does not have a permanent home; however, his trace was discovered mostly around the few islands in the Seto Inland Sea, the body of water separating Honshu, Shikoku, and Kyushu, three of the four main islands of Japan.
WEAKNESS
He can only appear after sunset and before sunrise. In other words, he can’t exist in the area where sun is in the sky. Because Sunshine would destroy his energy which comes from people’s dreams. In addition, Mr.Dream has a sweet tooth. He is addicted to sweet dreams. This weakness made him get into a lot of troubles, and one of them is that he exposed his trace in the human world a few times.
SUPERPOWER
Mr. Dream has no concrete body. This is one of his weakness though, it could be a superpower as well. It’s easier for him to sneak into people’s house and steal their dreams with invisible and flexible body. His main super power is that he is able to control people’s dreams. After a hundred years accumulation of energy, he obtains the access to get in and out of dreams as he wants. In the meantime, he can also decide the development of people’s dreams. For human being, the dream is a key connected to their unconsciousness. Through controlling people’s dreams, Mr,Dream indirectly controls people’s subconscious. Therefore, he plans to occupy the whole world step by step through controlling human being.
ENEMY
People who stay up late, especially the increasing number of younger people who don’t sleep at night. The phenomenon not only gains the risk he exposes himself in front of human being during the task, but also he will lose his energy source.
