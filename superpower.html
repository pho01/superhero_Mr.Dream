The event first happened in late 1996, a woman’s sweet dream was interrupted while she was sleeping at the midnight, then, she saw her sweet dream was eaten by a human-like something. After that, more and more similar situations were reported to the police successively. Therefore, National Security Agency assigned special detective PeiJung to deal with this freak condition. 21 years have passed away though, Mr.Dream has not even caught once. However, detective PeiJung gets the profound understanding toward Mr.Dream based on the 21 years research.
SUPERPOWER
Mr. Dream has no concrete body. This is one of his weakness though, it could be a superpower as well. It’s easier for him to sneak into people’s house and steal their dreams with invisible and flexible body. His main super power is that he is able to control people’s dreams. After a hundred years accumulation of energy, he obtains the access to get in and out of dreams as he wants. In the meantime, he can also decide the development of people’s dreams. For human being, the dream is a key connected to their unconsciousness. Through controlling people’s dreams, Mr,Dream indirectly controls people’s subconscious. Therefore, he plans to occupy the whole world step by step through controlling human being.
