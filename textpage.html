OBJECTIVE

The event first happened in late 1996, a woman’s sweet dream was interrupted while she was sleeping at the midnight, then, she saw her sweet dream was eaten by a human-like something. After that, more and more similar situations were reported to the police successively. Therefore, National Security Agency assigned special detective PeiJung to deal with this freak condition. 21 years have passed away though, Mr.Dream has not even caught once. However, detective PeiJung gets the profound understanding toward Mr.Dream based on the 21 years research.
